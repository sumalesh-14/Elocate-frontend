1 Kumar, A., et al.
"Smart E-waste Management System using IoT and Cloud Computing."
International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health. 2021. 		The authors developed a location-based service using GPS and Google Maps API to identify nearest e-waste collection centers. Used MongoDB for facility data storage and RESTful APIs for real-time tracking. This research informs our project's facility locator feature by demonstrating how geolocation services and map APIs can efficiently connect users with disposal centers, providing insights into real-time tracking implementation.
2 Zhang, Q., et al.
"E-waste Collection Facility Network Optimization and Management System."
Journal of Cleaner Production. 2022. 		Developed facility management system for e-waste collection centers with capacity tracking, certification verification, and real-time data synchronization. Used GIS mapping to optimize facility locations and coverage areas. Implemented REST APIs for facility registration and status updates. This research informs our facility management features, showing how to maintain verified facility databases, track collection center capacities, and ensure proper certification. Provides insights into facility network optimization for maximum coverage and accessibility.
3 Patel, K., & Desai, M.
"Gamification Strategies to Promote Sustainable E-waste Disposal Behavior."
Sustainability. 2021. 		Created gamification framework with point-based rewards, leaderboards, and achievement badges. Used behavioral psychology principles to increase participation by 67%. This study informs our rewards and incentive system, providing methodologies for user engagement through gamification, point systems, and social recognition to encourage recycling behavior.
4 Silva, R., & Costa, M.
"Circular Economy Ecosystem for Electronic Waste: Stakeholder Integration and Value Chain Analysis."
Resources, Conservation & Recycling. 2023. 		Analyzed e-waste circular economy ecosystem connecting consumers, NGOs, recyclers, and manufacturers. Developed multi-stakeholder platform with role-based workflows and value chain tracking. Demonstrated 35% increase in material recovery through ecosystem integration. This study provides framework for our multi-stakeholder ecosystem approach, validating our role-based system (Citizens, NGOs, Admins). Shows how connecting different actors in the e-waste value chain creates sustainable circular economy and improves overall recycling rates.
5 Chen, W., & Liu, Y.
"Design and Implementation of Multi-platform Waste Management Application."
International Journal of Computer Applications. 2022. 		Developed React-based web portal and React Native mobile apps with Spring Boot backend. Implemented offline mode with local storage and push notification system. This research validates our choice of Next.js for web and mobile app development, showing how responsive design and offline capabilities enhance user experience.
6 Anderson, T., et al.
"Visualizing Environmental Impact: Dashboard Design for Sustainable Behavior Tracking."
Journal of Environmental Psychology. 2021. 		Created environmental impact dashboard using D3.js and Chart.js to display CO2 savings, waste diverted, and resource recovery metrics. Users showed 45% increase in recycling frequency. This study informs our impact dashboard feature, providing methods to visualize carbon footprint reduction, material recovery, and environmental metrics to motivate continued user participation.

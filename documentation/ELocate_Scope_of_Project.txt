================================================================================
                        SCOPE OF THE PROJECT
================================================================================

The project aims to develop a comprehensive e-waste management platform that 
revolutionizes how citizens, NGOs, and businesses handle electronic waste 
disposal through smart technology integration, real-time facility tracking, 
and sustainable recycling practices. The scope of the project includes the 
following features:

1. Smart Facility Locator & Booking System: Uses GPS and MapBox/Leaflet APIs 
   to identify nearest certified e-waste collection centers with real-time 
   availability, and enables doorstep pickup scheduling with QR verification.

2. AI/ML Integration: Implements machine learning for automated device 
   recognition from photos and NLP-powered chatbot providing 24/7 support, 
   personalized recommendations, and guided disposal assistance.

3. Gamification & Impact Dashboard: Provides point-based rewards system with 
   leaderboards, and visualizes environmental impact including CO2 savings, 
   materials recovered, and carbon footprint reduction.

4. Multi-Stakeholder Role System: Implements secure role-based platform for 
   Citizens, NGOs, and Administrators with customized workflows, corporate 
   compliance tracking, and automated CSR reporting.

5. Circular Economy Ecosystem: Connects all stakeholders in sustainable value 
   chain with educational resources, certification programs, and analytics to 
   maximize material recovery and promote environmental awareness.

================================================================================
                            ELOCATE - PROJECT ABSTRACT
                     E-waste Management Platform - Executive Summary
================================================================================

ABSTRACT
========

ELocate is an AI-powered e-waste management platform connecting citizens with 
certified recycling facilities across India. Using Faster R-CNN for device 
recognition, Next.js/Spring Boot architecture, and MapBox integration, it 
addresses India's 3.2M tonnes annual e-waste crisis where only 10% is properly 
recycled. The system features role-based dashboards, AI chatbot assistance, 
gamification, and QR-verified pickups to increase recycling rates by 15-20% 
while ensuring compliance with E-Waste Management Rules 2016.


KEY HIGHLIGHTS
==============

1. AI-POWERED DEVICE RECOGNITION
   Faster R-CNN model delivers real-time device identification within 2-3 
   seconds via camera scanning with automatic disposal recommendations.

2. MULTI-STAKEHOLDER ECOSYSTEM
   Specialized dashboards for Citizens, NGOs, Recycling Facilities, and 
   Administrators with facility verification and booking management.

3. INTELLIGENT FACILITY MAPPING
   MapBox integration enables location-based discovery of certified e-waste 
   centers with real-time navigation and proximity filtering.

4. GAMIFICATION & IMPACT TRACKING
   Points-based rewards, leaderboards, and carbon footprint tracking (20kg 
   CO2 saved per device) with digital certificates for sustained engagement.

5. SECURE & COMPLIANT ARCHITECTURE
   Auth0 JWT authentication, RBAC, GDPR compliance, deployed on Vercel and 
   Railway with PostgreSQL/MongoDB ensuring 99.9% uptime.
